JOIN THE TEAM

Hi, we’re Carwow and if you’re looking for an opportunity to accelerate your career - we’d love to hear from you! We're not petrolheads, we just want to make car buying easy - so we’ve created an open & honest marketplace for buying and selling a car. As a fast moving, flexible team of 400 we work on either a hybrid or a fully remote basis (we’ve even been Flexified) Buy. Sell. Wow!


Carwow was founded in 2010 as the Rotten Tomatoes of the car review world. However, founders James, Alexandra and David soon realised they could do more than just help people decide which car to buy - they could also help them buy it! After successfully launching in the UK, Carwow went international - landing in Germany in 2016 and Spain in 2018


We've been on an exciting journey. Within the space of a year, we recruited 200 people to our team, opened a new Operations & Tech base in Portugal AND launched our 'sell your car' feature in the UK and Germany meaning users could sell their old car, as well as find their next. Now, we are focusing on growing this further as well as introducing some exciting new categories….watch this space.
